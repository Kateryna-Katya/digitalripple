Digital Ripple

Tap once, watch the whole board light up.

Digital Ripple's basically a puzzle game where you tap stuff and watch signals ripple across the board like dropping stones in water — it's pretty satisfying to watch everything light up.

Play Free Now

Features

  • Wave Interference Physics

    When signals crash into each other, they either boost each other up or cancel out completely. It's kinda like real wave physics, which makes the puzzles feel more... I dunno, logical?

  • 50+ Hand-Crafted Circuits

    We designed every level using real wave physics principles, so there's always a logical solution (no random trial-and-error BS).

  • Unlockable Signal Types

    Earn pulse, arc, and echo signals as you progress — and each type changes how the waves spread in cool ways.

  • Zero Ads, Zero Timers

    No energy bars, no interruptions. Pick up a puzzle and put it down whenever you want.

About

The whole idea came from me obsessing over this question: could we make a puzzle game that feels like dropping rocks in a pond? Turns out, yeah. We actually coded in real wave physics (took forever to get right), so playing it feels like you're messing around with ripples in your bathtub. No timers. No clutter. Just you, the grid, and the ripple.
