Privacy Policy

1. Scope

This Policy covers any personally identifiable information collected or stored by Digital Ripple (hereinafter referred to as the "Company").

2. Purpose

Company gathers personally identifiable information when you register for our games to serve the well-being of the game for which you have registered. Your personally identifiable information may be used for:
Statistics gathering to better understand our audience
Effectively reprimanding users who abuse or break the terms of our services
Detecting potential security loopholes
Solving technical problems

3. Details

Company makes every effort to minimize the amount of personally identifiable information that is automatically gathered by using our services. In addition to information you willingly provide through any of our products, we also store your IP address.

4. Disclosure

Your personally identifiable information will not be intentionally disclosed to any third parties except where required by law in the province of Ontario, Canada. We use third-party servers from:

  • LiquidWeb
  • Atlassian
  • Amazon

and are limited by the respective privacy policies and security safeguards put in place by these third parties.

5. Deletion

If you wish for your personally identifiable information to be permanently deleted from our records, you may contact us at contact@digitalripple.space.

6. Protection

Company acknowledges the value of your privacy and uses safeguards to ensure the security of your personally identifiable information. If we discover that the security of your data has been compromised, you will be contacted via email, if available.

7. Note

Communications you make with other users in our game chats are viewable by others. You should not disclose personal information using our service.

8. Alteration

Company reserves the right to modify this policy at any time for any reason.
